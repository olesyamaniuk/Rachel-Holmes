About the Game

Rachel Holmes: Differences is a fast-paced puzzle game that challenges players to become the ultimate detective. Dive into beautifully illustrated scenes, search for hidden differences, and solve intriguing mysteries to help Rachel Holmes crack her cases. With time-limited levels, competitive multiplayer, and captivating storylines, this game offers a unique twist on the classic "spot the difference" experience.

features

  • Detective Adventure

    Immerse yourself in a mystery-filled world as Rachel Holmes, a top-tier detective.

  • Challenging Levels

    Hundreds of beautifully designed levels with increasing difficulty.

  • Real-Time Multiplayer

    Compete with other players to spot differences the fastest.

  • Captivating Artwork

    Stunning hand-drawn scenes that bring each mystery to life.

  • Progressive Challenges

    Unlock new storylines and cases as you advance through the game.

  • Offline Mode

    Play anywhere, anytime, even without an internet connection.

How to Play

  • Spot the Differences

    Carefully examine two images and find subtle differences before time runs out.

  • Compete in Multiplayer Mode

    Challenge friends or players worldwide in exciting real-time competitions

  • Unlock New Cases

    Progress through levels to uncover mysteries and unlock thrilling new cases

  • Score High

    The faster you spot differences, the higher your score! Compete to climb the leaderboard.

  • Use Hints and Tools

    Stuck? Use magnifying tools or hints to find tricky differences.
